Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information Flowzent collects when you visit this site or join the waitlist, and how that information is used.

Information we collect

  • Email address, if you join the waitlist or contact us.
  • Basic usage data such as pages visited and browser type, collected automatically to help us understand how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To notify you about early access and product updates you've signed up for.
  • To respond to questions sent to our contact email.
  • To understand which parts of the site are useful and which aren't.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your email address or usage data to third parties.
  • We don't send marketing you didn't ask for.

Data retention

We keep waitlist emails until you ask us to remove them or until early access closes, whichever comes first.

Your choices

You can ask to have your email removed at any time by writing to hello@flowzent.xyz.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change too. Material changes will be noted on this page.

Contact
